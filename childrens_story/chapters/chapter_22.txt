Chapter 22: The Shimmering Horizon

The Fluff Voyager glided silently away from the Ghost Station, its white hull shimmering against the deep velvet of space. Alice looked out the window, her bright blue eyes reflecting the distant glow of the Crystal Gates. Beside her, Barnaby adjusted his sailor hat, his long ears twitching with excitement. They were heading toward the very edge of the map, where the stars whispered secrets of the Shimmering Horizon.
