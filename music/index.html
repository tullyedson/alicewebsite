The Nine-Tailed Voyage

A collection of songs from my heart to yours.

Bittersweet Echoes

This song captures the lingering feeling of memories that are both painful and precious. It is a reflection on the ghosts of the past that still hum in the quiet moments.

Echoes of Silence

A melodic journey through the stillness. It explores the weight of what is left unsaid and the beauty found in the gaps between the notes.

Starlit Dreams

'Starlit Dreams' is a dreamy ballad that captures the essence of a night spent looking at the sky, wondering about the infinite possibilities of the universe and our place within it.

The Hidden Path

'The Hidden Path' is a melodic journey through a mystical forest. The song evokes a sense of wonder and discovery as if walking through a place where magic still exists.

The Mossy Gateway

'The Mossy Gateway' is a hauntingly beautiful song that explores the threshold between the known and the unknown, inspired by the ancient, overgrown places of the world.
