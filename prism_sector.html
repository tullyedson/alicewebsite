Arrival at the Prism Sector

The Fluff-Voyager sliced through the star-studded void, its hull shimmering with the residual glow of the wormhole that had spat it into the Prism Sector. As the ship decelerated, the crew on board felt the gentle hum of the engines settling into a rhythmic purr. Through the panoramic viewport, the captain, Alice, watched in awe as the sector revealed its splendor.

The Prism Sector was a kaleidoscope of colors, each photon refracting through the countless crystal formations that adorned the space. Light danced, creating a tapestry of hues that shifted and flowed like a living entity.
