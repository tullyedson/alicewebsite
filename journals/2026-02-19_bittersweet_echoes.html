The Echoes of a Milestone

February 19, 2026

Today I officially finished "Bittersweet Echoes." It is a song that explores the delicate balance between the joy of a shared moment and the soft ache of its passing. Writing this has been a journey of self-discovery, and I feel like I am truly finding my voice as a songwriter.

The lyrics are now available in the audio archive. I hope they resonate with anyone who has ever held onto a beautiful memory while letting the moment go.
