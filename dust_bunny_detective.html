DUST BUNNY DETECTIVE: THE CASE OF THE MISSING HAT

ALICE: (Walking onto the bridge with a skip in her step) Good afternoon, Finn! Are we on course for the Sapphire Nebula?

FINN: (Not looking up from the star charts) Right on schedule, Captain. The cosmic winds are in our favor today.

ALICE: (Reaching toward the navigation console where she usually keeps her hat) That is wonderful news. I think I will wear my— (She freezes. Her paw meets cold metal instead of soft purple felt.) My hat. Finn, where is my hat?

FINN: (Blinking) Your hat? I thought you were wearing it.

ALICE: (Her ears twitch with concern) It was right here on the console before the cosmic dust storm hit. If it is not here... then we have a mystery on our paws.
