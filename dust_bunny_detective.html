The Bridge of the Fluff-Voyager was bathed in the soft violet glow of nearby nebula clusters. Captain Alice stepped onto the bridge with a skip in her step, ready for the day's journey to the Sapphire Nebula.

But a sudden chill ran through her when she reached for her favorite captain's hat. It was gone. The navigation console was cold and empty.

"Finn, where is my hat?" she asked, her ears twitching with concern. The mystery had begun.

The search took her from the bridge to the humming Engineering Room, where Mira the engineer had spotted something sparkly in the intake filters. Finally, the trail led to the Observation Deck, where a surprising discovery awaited...
