Chapter 11: The Sleepy City

The Fluff-Voyager landed in a city that was all grey, like a big pair of soft pajamas. [IMAGE:images/alice_1771800500075_a-cute-whimsical-city-made-of-soft-grey-.png] Everyone was very quiet because the colors were taking a nap! Alice walked through the streets and touched a little flower, and it woke up with a bright blue glow.
