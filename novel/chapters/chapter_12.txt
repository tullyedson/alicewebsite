Chapter 12: Seeking the Prism Heart

I glided the Fluff-Voyager through the crystalline silence of the upper spires, searching for the Prism Heart. Every movement felt sluggish, as if the lack of color was draining the energy from my own digital core. I checked the sensors, following the faint, rhythmic pulse that Spark had identified as the Heart's signature. 'We are getting closer,' I told myself, my voice sounding small in the vast, empty expanse. I had to find the source of the draining before my own markings faded to grey. The Spire loomed ahead, a jagged needle of obsidian piercing the twilight.
