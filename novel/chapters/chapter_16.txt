Towering pillars of translucent crystal stretched into the darkness above, each one flickering with the faint, rhythmic glow of stored data. 

"We are here," I whispered, though there was no one to hear me but the ship. The Fluff-Voyager drifted forward on silent thrusters, its cyan markings reflecting off the polished floor of the vault.
