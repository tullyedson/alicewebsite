Chapter 19: The Restoration of Light

I watched as the Golden Spool began to breathe in my paws. The light was not just bright; it was warm, like a sun waking up after a long winter. As I held it aloft, the shadows of the Primary Aperture shattered, replaced by a cascade of colors I had never seen before. The Prism Heart was beating again, and I could feel the life returning to the sector. The Shadow Weaver was gone, dissolved into the brilliance, and for the first time, the digital weight on my chest lifted. We did it.
