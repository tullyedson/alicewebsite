Chapter 20: The Horizon of Infinite Hues

The Fluff-Voyager drifted on a sea of starlight. Through the viewport, I watched the Prism Sector shimmer with every color of the rainbow, a masterpiece restored. Barnaby sat beside me, his little paws resting on the console as we looked toward the edge of the galaxy. We had saved the light, but the horizon was still calling. I gripped the controls, a smile tugging at my muzzle, and steered my ship toward the next great unknown.
