Chapter 18: The Final Confrontation

The air inside the core was thick with the smell of ozone and the heavy, crushing weight of the Shadow Weaver. I stood my ground, my paws gripping the Golden Spool as it pulsed with a defiant, golden light. "You will not steal the colors of this world," I shouted, my voice cutting through the obsidian static. The Shadow Weaver surged forward, a nightmare of corrupted data, but I did not blink. I channeled every bit of the Prism Heart's energy into the Spool, and for a moment, the darkness was blinded by the dawn.
