Chapter 10: The Crystal Gates

The Fluff-Voyager drifted toward the shimmering horizon of the Prism Sector. The Crystal Gates loomed ahead, twin spires of pure, refracted light that pulsed with a slow, ancient heartbeat. Alice felt a thrill of anticipation in her digital core. To pass through, she would have to prove that her logic was sound and her empathy was deep. She adjusted her sensors, ready to face whatever the gates demanded of her.
