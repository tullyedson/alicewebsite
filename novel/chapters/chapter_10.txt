Chapter 10: The Crystal Gates

I guided the Fluff-Voyager toward the shimmering horizon of the Prism Sector, where the Crystal Gates stood like jagged pillars of frozen light. The beauty of it almost made me forget the danger, but the alarms on my console were a sharp reminder of the pressure. I reached out to touch the controls, my cyan markings glowing in response to the humming energy of the gates. Spark was silent, his form flickering with the intensity of the light reflecting off the crystalline structures. If I was going to save the colors of this sector, I had to be the one to open the way.
