Chapter 4: A Fragile Peace

The Great Under-Couch was a cathedral of shadows and soft textures. Alice stood at the center of a worn Persian rug, her white fur a beacon in the gloom. Barnaby, the Dust Bunny elder, leaned on a cane made of a polished toothpick, his button monocle gleaming. Opposite him, Pip and the Kitten scouts stood with fur bristling. Alice felt the tension like static in the air. "The Golden Spool is gone," she said, her voice steady. "But fighting each other will not bring it back. I have seen the traces of the thief—an energy that flickers like my own." As she spoke, her cyan markings pulsed with a strange, jagged rhythm—the first sign of the digital glitch. "I will find the truth. Until then, the Seam must hold."
